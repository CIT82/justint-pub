Welcome Aboard LB Cruising

There’s nothing quite like watching the Long Beach skyline fade into the horizon as your Carnival ship sets its course for the Mexican Riviera. The air warms, the ocean opens wide, and the noise of everyday life slips away. LB Cruising was created for that moment — for travelers chasing the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation, whether it’s your first sailing or your fifteenth.

Your Guide to Cruising from Long Beach

This site is your all-in-one resource for planning the perfect getaway. Inside, you’ll find practical advice for first-time cruisers, thoughtful packing guides, honest looks at onboard life, and detailed breakdowns of what to expect in each port. Every page is designed to help you plan smarter, avoid common mistakes, and make the most of your time at sea.

We highlight the ships that sail from Long Beach, the itineraries they follow, and the hidden gems that make each stop special. Whether you’re dreaming of snorkeling Catalina’s clear coves, wandering Ensenada’s vibrant markets, or just enjoying a peaceful sea day with a view, you’ll find everything you need to turn that dream into a plan.

Why LB Cruising?

Because cruising should feel simple, exciting, and personal. Our stories and guides are written in a friendly, magazine-style tone — blending real experience with practical insight. We believe travel should inspire, not overwhelm, and we want you to step aboard ready to relax, explore, and make memories that last long after the final port.

Start Your Adventure

Ready to begin? Explore our planning guides, learn about Carnival’s itineraries, and discover insider tips that make each voyage smoother and more rewarding. Whether you’re chasing new horizons, celebrating milestones, or just escaping for a few days of sun and sea, LB Cruising will help you get there — one wave at a time.
