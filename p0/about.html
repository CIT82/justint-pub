About LB Cruising

LB Cruising was born from the simple thrill of a first sail away. Standing on deck, watching the Long Beach skyline fade into the horizon, I realized that cruising wasn’t just another vacation option—it was a lifestyle full of discovery, relaxation, and unexpected connections. What started as a personal adventure quickly became a passion worth sharing. This site is my way of capturing that experience and helping others chart their own course at sea.

Our Mission

The mission of LB Cruising is simple: to make cruising approachable, exciting, and easy to plan. Whether you’re stepping onboard for the very first time or looking for ways to make your tenth voyage more memorable, we aim to be your trusted guide. Through first-hand stories, practical tips, and honest reflections, we highlight the moments that matter most—sunrises on deck, laughter at dinner, the quiet peace of the open sea, and yes, even the lessons learned from overpacking or overspending.

Why Long Beach?

We focus on cruises sailing from Long Beach, California, because that’s where the journey began. From the iconic Carnival Radiance to the larger-than-life Panorama, Long Beach has become a hub for West Coast cruising. It’s a port filled with history, energy, and easy access for travelers across the region. By highlighting this departure point, we get to showcase itineraries that balance adventure—like Baja Mexico getaways—with relaxation, all within reach of home.

What You’ll Find Here

  • First-Hand Blogs: Stories of first-time cruising, packing challenges, life onboard, and money lessons learned the real way—by experience.
  • Planning & Tips: Landing pages that link to in-depth guides so you can prepare with confidence.
  • Ship & Port Guides: Overviews that help you know what to expect before you sail.
  • Inspiration: Photos, taglines, and glimpses of the cruising lifestyle that spark the imagination.

Our goal isn’t to overwhelm you with data—it’s to give you the kind of guidance you’d get from a well-traveled friend who’s been there, made mistakes, and come back with stories worth sharing.

Who This Site Is For

Maybe you’re planning your very first getaway and feeling a little unsure. Maybe you’ve cruised before but want to get more out of your next voyage. Or maybe you just like daydreaming about where the horizon leads. Wherever you are, this site is for you. We believe cruising should feel accessible, whether you’re traveling solo, as a couple, with family, or as part of a group. Everyone deserves to step onboard confident and ready to enjoy the journey.

Looking Ahead

LB Cruising is still growing, just like any traveler learning the ropes. The more we sail, the more we’ll add: detailed port reviews, excursion spotlights, ship comparisons, and tips for making the most of every cruise dollar. What won’t change is our perspective: real, honest storytelling from the deck, not just the brochure.

At the end of the day, cruising is about more than itineraries and ship specs. It’s about the moments you carry home: sunsets that leave you speechless, conversations with strangers who become friends, and the simple joy of watching the shoreline disappear. That’s what LB Cruising is all about—helping you get there, and savoring the journey along the way.
